Entering Scoring Mode

Who can score?

Anyone with the team's scoring password can log stats for a live game. The scoring screen is fully integrated into the standard parent app.

Navigate to the Game

Open the Eggbeater app and find the game you are about to score on the Schedule or Scores tab.

Tap Score Game

Tap the Score Game (✏️) button on the game card to open the Scoring Console.

Enter Password

You will be prompted to enter the scoring password. If you don't know the password, ask your team's coach or club administrator.

2

Pre-Game Setup

Setting Cap Colors

Before the game starts, verify that your team has the correct cap color assigned. By standard logic, Eggbeater places White caps on the left side and Dark caps on the right side of the screen. If it's incorrect, use the toggle tools at the top to fix it.

Setting the Roster

Your team's entire imported roster will be visible in the player panel. Tap the names of players who are present to activate them for the game. Grayed-out players will not be available for you to apply stats to.

Assign the Starting Goalie

At the bottom of the roster panel, locate your starting goalkeeper and tap the Assign Goalkeeper (🧤) badge next to their name. This is critical because Goalie-specific stats (like Saves) are tracked differently than field player stats.

3

Recording Stats

The Scoring Interface

The scoring console is built for highly efficient tap interactions so you can keep your eyes on the pool.

Logging Goals & Assists

  1. Tap the name of the player who scored.
  2. Tap green Goal prominently displayed on their stat sheet.
  3. Eggbeater will immediately prompt you for the Assist. Tap the assisting player's name, or select "No Assist" if unassisted.

Logging Exclusions

If a player is ejected, tap their name and select Exclusion.

If your team drew an exclusion on the opposing team (earning a man-up advantage), tap your player who drew the foul and select Earned Excl. This stat highlights key offensive contributors.

Other Key Stats

Missed ShotsTap the shooting player and select Shot/Miss to record an attempt.
StealsTap the defending player and select Steal.
Goalie SavesGoalie stats automatically map to the active Goalkeeper. Simply tap Save when they block a shot.
4

Game Management

Clock & Quarters

Use the Start/Stop clock tools at the top to mirror the real scoreboard. This helps parents following remotely feel the live flow of the game! When a quarter finishes, tap End Q1 (or the respective quarter) to progress the match segment. At the very end, tap End Game.

Correcting Mistakes

Scoring mistakes happen! At the bottom of the scoring module is a rolling Event Log of all play-by-play actions throughout the game. If you accidentally credited a goal or foul to the wrong player, scroll to it in the event log, tap Undo, and log it again properly.

📡

Live Sync is Automatic: Every time you log an event or start the clock, the Eggbeater cloud syncs the new dashboard back to all remote parents within ~100 milliseconds!
