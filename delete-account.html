Delete Account & Data

How to request deletion of your Eggbeater account and associated data.

How to Request Deletion

1

Sign out of the app

Open Eggbeater → Settings → Account → Sign Out.

2

Send a deletion request

Email us from the address associated with your account. Include your name and the email address you used to sign in.

3

We process your request

We will delete your account and associated data within 30 days and confirm by email.

Email Deletion Request

What Data Is Deleted

Data Type Action Retention
Google account sign-in and account ID Deleted Within 30 days
Email address Deleted Within 30 days
Saved preferences (age group, calendar connection) Deleted Within 30 days
Push notification token Deleted Within 30 days
Tournament & game data (scores, schedules) Kept Club-owned data, not personal

Note: The app can be used without creating an account. If you never signed in, no personal data was collected.
