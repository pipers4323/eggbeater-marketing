How It Works

Up and running in minutes

No spreadsheets, no group texts. Just a clean app your whole club will actually use.

1

Set up your club

Create your club profile, add your colors and logo, and invite your coaching staff. Your branded app is live immediately.

2

Add players & teams

Import your roster or invite players directly. Organize into age groups and teams. Players download the app and they're in.

3

Run your season

Post the schedule, send push notifications, and manage everything from the admin dashboard.

Features

Everything your club needs

Purpose-built for water polo. Not another generic sports app.

🏊

Multi-Club Support

Run multiple clubs or age groups under one account. Each club gets its own branded experience — separate colors, logos, and rosters — all managed from a single admin dashboard.

🔔

Push Notifications

Instantly notify players and spectators about game changes, practice updates, or club announcements.

📊

Bracket Sheet Wizard

Paste a Google Sheets link and Eggbeater does the rest — it reads your pool seedings, scans the schedule for your team's games, and deploys a fully structured bracket to the app in seconds. No re-entry, no copy-paste errors.

📋

Roster Management

Full player profiles with positions, numbers, and contact info. Keep your roster up to date across the season.

🎨

Club Branding

Your colors, your logo. Each club's app looks and feels uniquely theirs — no generic templates.

📱

Native Mobile App

Available on iOS and Android. Coaches and players get a fast, polished experience — not a web page in a frame.

🏆

Junior Olympic Mode

Built for USA Water Polo's National Junior Olympics. Paste the MASTER BY DIVISION sheet, pick your division and team name, and Eggbeater maps every possible bracket path across all four days — group play in the Schedule tab, the full bracket tree in the Bracket tab. Auto-syncs every few minutes so spectators see confirmed opponents the moment results post.

🔴

Live Scoring

Real-time scoring from poolside with goal, assist, steal, exclusion, forced ball under, field block, and goalie-save tracking. Spectators follow the live score, event log, and full box score — updating every few seconds. Works offline too, so a bad pool connection won't lose a single goal.

⭐

My Player Tracking

Families can follow their player from the Roster tab and keep goals, assists, steals, exclusions, specialty shots, and advanced stats one tap away across the whole tournament.

📚

History & Season Stats

Completed games move automatically into History with scores, box scores, and season record summaries, so spectators can catch up quickly even if they missed a game live.

📱

Lock Screen Live Activities

On iPhone, the game score appears right on the lock screen and in the Dynamic Island — team logos, running clock, and live event feed without opening the app. On Android 16, a persistent Live Update chip keeps the score one glance away.

🧩

Home Screen Widgets

Score, schedule, and stats widgets for iOS and Android. Spectators glance at their home screen to see the live score, next game time, or top scorers — no need to open anything.

⌚

Apple Watch Companion

Spectators can follow a selected team or game from their wrist with club-branded colors, a Featured Game card, Auto Next Game, Team Schedule, and goal haptics for live score changes.

📅

Calendar Subscribe

Spectators subscribe once to a WebCal feed and every game appears in Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, or Outlook — auto-updating as times, locations, and bracket opponents change. No re-syncing needed.
