How It Works

Up and running in minutes

No spreadsheets, no group texts. Just a clean app your whole club will actually use.

1

Set up your club

Create your club profile, add your colors and logo, and invite your coaching staff. Your branded app is live immediately.

2

Add players & teams

Import your roster or invite players directly. Organize into age groups and teams. Players download the app and they're in.

3

Run your season

Post the schedule, track attendance, send push notifications, and manage everything from the admin dashboard.
